Generative AI x Data Analytics Workshop

30 Nov 2024
Bangkok, Thailand
30 Participants

Workshop Overview

This hands-on workshop combined generative AI prompting techniques with data analytics to help businesses extract more value from their data. Participants learned how to effectively leverage large language models (LLM) from tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude for many business-related tasks and in particular enhance their data analysis workflows and generate actionable insights.

Key Topics Covered

  • Introduction to generative AI models and large language models (LLM)
  • Applications of generative AI models in data analytics for business
  • M.A.S.T.E.R prompting framework for effective results
  • Examples of practical use cases, including writing job descriptions, generating marketing copy, and automating data analysis workflows
  • Features of ChatGPT and Gemini
